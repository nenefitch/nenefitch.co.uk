About Nene Fitch

Nene Fitch is a team of experienced change professionals specialising in business transformation for ambitious, high-growth businesses and global enterprises. We help our clients navigate complex change—whether strategic, operational, technological, or regulatory—by optimising processes, enhancing efficiency, and embedding lasting improvements.

We have led complex, industry-first transformations that have been subject to intense scrutiny from boards, shareholders, regulators, and the media. Our expertise ensures that high-profile change is delivered with precision, resilience, and transparency—meeting the highest standards of governance and stakeholder expectations.

Our consultants have led transformation programmes for banking, insurance, retail, and other sectors, driving efficiency, innovation, and compliance. We support organisations in strategic transformation by defining target operating models, streamlining workflows, and aligning change initiatives with business objectives to maximise impact.

    Mobilisation and Team Building – We establish governance, define programme scope, lead requirements analysis, and build high-performing teams to drive transformation. Once the foundation is in place, we seamlessly transition execution to client teams.

    Full End-to-End Delivery – We provide a Programme Director and an expert delivery team to manage transformation from strategy to execution. This includes designing target operating models, integrating technology solutions, and developing training programmes to embed change.

    Programme Governance & Risk Control – We implement robust governance structures, ensuring clear risk management, planning, and compliance frameworks. Our approach enables businesses to maintain control and transparency throughout transformation.

    Programme Communication & Engagement – Effective change requires alignment at every level of an organisation. We develop tailored communication strategies to engage stakeholders, drive adoption, and ensure that teams understand and embrace the transformation journey.

    In-Flight Health Check – If your transformation is already underway, we offer independent reviews and assurance, assessing planning, budgets, governance, and risk controls to ensure alignment with business goals.

Clear, informative and to the point 

Management Information is a key part of stakeholder management in any programme. Without a structured approach and format, MI can be unclear and (worse still) leave it’s readers disengaged and wondering "so what?" 

Using a proven toolset, Nene Fitch start by identifying the right audience and determining what updates are pertinent to them.  Where decisions need to be made, we back them up with clear evidence and recommendations.  Where decisions have been made, we provide a clear audit trail which is vital in the event of audit or regulatory investigations.
